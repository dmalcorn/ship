Bonus
My recommendation: Radar or ScanSearch
Radar best communicates "monitoring agent that scans your project" — it's distinctive, not used elsewhere in Ship's icon rail, and maps to the Dependabot mental model (automated scanning).

ScanSearch is the simpler, more readable alternative — the scanner frame + magnifying glass is universally understood as "finding things."

Avoid Bot/Sparkles — they signal "AI chatbot" which is exactly the mental model we're trying to avoid. FleetGraph is a monitoring agent, not a conversational assistant.
